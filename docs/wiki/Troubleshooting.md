# Troubleshooting

## Unsupported Link

Try a direct media page URL. Short links, private links, login-only links, or unsupported sites may fail.

## No Downloadable Media

Switch quality to Original and try again. Some sites do not expose all requested formats.

## Region or Age Restrictions

The app cannot bypass site restrictions. Try a link that is publicly available in your region and does not require sign-in.

## Rate Limiting

If a site rate limits requests, wait and retry later.

## Diagnostics

Use the About button in the app to export logs. Logs are designed to redact pasted links to host and length information.
